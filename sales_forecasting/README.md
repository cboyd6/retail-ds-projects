## Retail Sales Performance

### Goal
Use sales data to show the performance that promotional sales has on total sales.


### Questions
Would having a single item on sales in customers cart shows an increase in cart size, ie does promotions drive more sales due to additional purchases?

## Data requirements
We will need data that tell use breakdown of each item in cart when a purchase was made during a sale.

Columns needed
> cart id, customer id, product id, quantity, date, regular price, sale price
